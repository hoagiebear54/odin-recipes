Chicken Parm Omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • Â½ cup milk
  • A handful of shredded parmesan
  • 2 chicken tenderloins (cut into thin strips)
  • Red chilli sliced
  • 2 green onions sliced
  • 2 tbspns olive oil

Recipe

  1. In a mixing bowl crack two eggs, add some milk and whisk.
  2. Heat a non-stick grill pan and grill chicken strips until they are white and half cooked.
  3. Now heat a non-stick frying pan, spray some oil on it and when it is medium hot pour egg mixture onto it. Move the pan and make the omelette round.
  4. -When it starts to change colour sprinkle cheese, grilled chicken, chillies and spring onion. Fold it and flip the side.
  5. Take it onto a plate and serve hot with toasted wholemeal bread.
